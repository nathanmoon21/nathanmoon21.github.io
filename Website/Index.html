Some Information About Myself/center>

My name is Robert Nathaniel Moon. I am from Buena Vista Georgia, and am a Junior at the University of Georgia. I am studying for a Finance degree and will apply to Terry College next semester. After getting my degree I intend on either working in Atlanta, or my family is constantly insistent that I work with them. I am still uncertain of what job I want so only time will tell.

My interests include country music, cars, and anything outdoors. My boyfriend and I love to go hiking and enjoy driving to the North Georgia Mountains and to Highlands North Carolina. Due to growing up in the middle of nowhere, I love any rural location.

I am hoping to get my degree from UGA and then I intend on entering the work force. I am considering going to Law School, however I want to gain some real world work experience before I make the decision to pursue it. Currently I work downtown bartending at a restaurant called The Office.
